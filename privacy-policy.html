Privacy Policy for PocketIndex

Last Updated: June 20, 2025

What Data We Store

PocketIndex stores data locally on your device only:

  • Your favorite Pokémon list
  • Your team Pokémon (up to 6)
  • Extension preferences

What We Don't Collect

  • No personal information
  • No browsing data
  • No tracking or analytics
  • No data sent to our servers

Third-Party Services

  • PokeAPI: We fetch Pokémon data from PokeAPI.co
  • No personal data is shared with PokeAPI
  • Requests are made directly from your browser

Your Control

  • All data stays on your device
  • Uninstalling removes all data
  • You can clear favorites/team anytime through the extension

Changes

Updates to this policy will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

PocketIndex uses local storage only. Your data remains private and under your complete control.
